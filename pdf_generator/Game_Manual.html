Urban Glow Grid

Operator Quick Guide

Your objective is to balance the city's energy demand across 6 iconic zones. Prove your efficiency in under 3 minutes.

1
Initialize System

Press the START button on the console to begin. You have exactly 3 minutes.

The city's power grid will immediately become unstable, and the 6 zones will start offline.

▶ PRESS START

2
Connect Zones

Gain control of the grid by connecting to all 6 zones. Turn each physical dial until the zone's LED indicator flashes GREEN.

Once connected, your dial actively controls that zone's energy output.

→ ● CONNECTED

3
Balance The Grid

The city's total energy load fluctuates constantly. Watch the OLED screen.

Adjust your dials to keep the consumption indicator (=) safely between the 2 vertical limit markers (|).

[--|======|--]

4
Prevent Blackouts

If a zone is poorly optimized, it will disconnect and its LED will flash RED.

You must quickly turn its dial to reconnect it before the overall grid balance fails.

→ ● RECONNECT!

🏆 Mission Success

  • CONNECT ALL ZONES: Ensure all 6 zones are active (Green).
  • MAINTAIN BALANCE: Keep the grid perfectly stable (between limits) for 5 consecutive seconds.
  • SURVIVE: Complete these tasks before the 3-minute timer runs out.
Speedrun Score = Remaining Time

⚠️ System Failure

  • TIME OUT: The 3-minute timer expires before you stabilize the grid.
  • OVERLOAD (> 80%): The total energy exceeds the maximum limit (right side of the gauge).
  • UNDERLOAD (< 25%): The total energy drops below the minimum limit (left side of the gauge).
You have 2.5 seconds to fix limit breaches!
