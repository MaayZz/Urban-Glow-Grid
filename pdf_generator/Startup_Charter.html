Startup Charter

Startup: CleanTech • Project: The Urban Glow-Grid

Commitment Statement: As a team, we define and assign formal roles to each member based on a standard startup structure. Each member has a clear role and physically or digitally signs this charter to confirm their responsibilities and commitment to the team in bringing The Urban Glow-Grid to life. This structure ensures accountability, improves coordination, and provides a clear identity within the project.
Key Words
Smart City Energy Mindfulness Interactive Simulation
1-Line Project Summary
“In order to promote energy mindfulness, our project Urban Glow-Grid allows citizens and students to balance a city's power grid through real-time interactive simulations, contrary to abstract electricity bills and static energy saving brochures.”
