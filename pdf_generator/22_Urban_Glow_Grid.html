Project Summary
“Our project, Urban Glow Grid, promotes energy mindfulness by allowing citizens and students to actively balance a city's power grid through engaging, real-time interactive simulations. We aim to replace abstract electricity bills and static energy-saving brochures with a tangible, gamified experience that demonstrates the real-world impact of urban energy management.”
Chosen Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)
  • SDG 7
    Affordable and Clean Energy Promoting energy mindfulness to actively reduce consumption and manage electric loads in real time.
  • SDG 11
    Sustainable Cities and Communities Optimizing urban resources via an interactive, dynamic layout representing city districts.
  • SDG 13
    Climate Action Educating end-users on reducing their carbon footprint through practical and simulated energy distribution management.
Major Team Changes for Investors / Mentors
1. Development Shift: We have transitioned our technical focus away from complex procedural generation in favor of a polished, architecturally realistic simulation of Paris. This shift ensures a more stable, visually stunning, and highly responsive user experience.

2. Role Formalization: Our team responsibilities have been formalized to enhance accountability. We have established clear leadership, technology, and artistic roles, ensuring streamlined communication and improved project delivery efficiency as we approach the next development milestones.
