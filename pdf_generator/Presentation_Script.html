Project: Urban Glow Grid · Duration: 5 Minutes · Team 22

0:00 - 0:45
ABIR - [SLIDE 1: TITLE]
(Wait for the slide to load, address the jury with energy)
"Hello everyone. Today, we are presenting how generative AI has transformed our project, Urban Glow Grid. More than just a tool, AI has been a true innovation partner for us, allowing us to push the boundaries of rapid prototyping."
0:45 - 1:30
ABIR - [SLIDE 2: COLLABORATIVE PARTNER]
"Our approach is built on human-machine synergy. AI didn't just draft text; it served as a technical scaffold. We increased our prototyping speed by 5x on the digital layer, while maintaining a permanent human filter to ensure the physical feasibility of our ideas."
1:30 - 2:15
SOFIANE - [SLIDE 3: LYRA METHODOLOGY]
(Point to the 4-D steps on the slide)
"To structure this collaboration, we developed the Lyra framework. It's a 4-step methodology—Deconstruct, Diagnose, Develop, Deliver—that allows us to extract high-precision results from AI."

"By assigning it the role of 'Technical Systems Director,' we transformed vague prompts into actionable blueprints."
2:15 - 3:15
SOFIANE - [SLIDE 4: DIGITAL & SLIDE 5: PHYSICAL]
"Concretely, for the digital part, AI generated the 'breathing' logic for the LEDs, using CSS variables for an organic feel. However, for the physical part, we had to correct its suggestions. It proposed a 1:10,000 scale, impossible for our 80cm board. We manually converged to 1:15,000, illustrating our role as a critical 'safeguard'."
3:15 - 4:15
MOHAMED - [SLIDE 6: DIVERGE & CONVERGE]
(Refer to the 'GLOW' winner)
"In creative development, AI was our brainstorming engine. After generating 10 different game mechanics—the Divergence phase—we converged on the Glow Intensity mechanic."

"This choice was deliberate: it relies on cognitive psychology by focusing on positive reinforcement. The city lights up when the user saves energy, making sustainability visually rewarding."
4:15 - 5:00
ABIR - [SLIDE 7: HUMAN FILTER & SLIDE 8: Q&A]
"In conclusion, AI gives us speed, but we provide the direction. Urban Glow Grid is the result of a balance between algorithmic innovation and human curation."
