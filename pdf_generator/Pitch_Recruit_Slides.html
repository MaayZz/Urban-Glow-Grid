What Is Urban Glow Grid?

The Project
Cities waste energy.
We make it visible.

Urban Glow Grid is an interactive installation that simulates a city's power grid in real time. Users physically control which districts receive energy — seeing the immediate environmental and social impact of every decision. It turns abstract electricity data into a tangible, gamified experience aligned with SDG 7, 11 & 13.

🎮 Gamified Simulation

Players toggle city districts (Haussmann, La Défense, Monuments) and watch the power grid react in real time.

💡 IoT + LED Hardware

Physical LED panels mirror the digital simulation — a true interactive installation experience.

🏙️ 3D Paris Replica

A detailed Three.js-powered 3D model of Paris serves as the visual interface of our system.
